Announcement November 19, 2024

Huddle 2.0 is here.

2 min read The Huddle Team
✦ Summary

A year-long wait is over. Huddle 2.0 lands with 100+ new features, a tripled user base in week one, and big things still to come - including Android.

Welcome to Huddle 2.0, Huddlers. A year-long wait has finally paid off, with over 100 new features to explore.

Over the past week, we tripled our user base and experienced multiple server crashes due to the influx of new Huddlers. We appreciate your patience - and the overwhelming love. The response has been incredible.

Thank you for your amazing messages in the app and your constructive suggestions on our Discord at discord.gg/huddle. We truly appreciate each and every one of you and take all feedback seriously as we strive to deliver the best Huddle experience possible.

In the coming weeks, we'll keep working on existing bugs and will also be sharing new blog posts to walk you through some of the exciting features in Huddle 2.0.

More news about the Android version will be released soon. For now, it's not meant for public release - only invited users or mutuals will have access to the alpha build. That's it for now. Have fun huddling!

Download on the App Store Try on Android (Alpha)

- The Huddle Team
