Product Update May 23, 2025

Mutuals-Only Posting Is Finally Here!

3 min read The Huddle Team
✦ Summary

The most-requested feature from the Huddle community is now live - post privately to only your mutuals, with a revamped Journals experience that unlocks more character space, photos, videos, and threaded comments.

You asked. You waited. We listened. Mutuals-Only Posting is here - the most requested feature from our community - now live and fully integrated into an upgraded Journals experience. Update your Huddle app to the latest version on both the iOS App Store and Google Play Store to get it now.

What's New in Journals

We've taken everything you loved about Journals and made it more powerful, more expressive, and more private:

  • Character limit raised from 369 to 2,000 - say what you actually want to say.
  • Photo and video attachments are now live - share moments alongside your words.
  • Comments and threads - your mutuals can now engage and respond more deeply in your journals.

When you select the Journal option while creating a post, only your mutuals - the people you're already friends with - will be able to see it. It's the perfect space for intimate, personal sharing.

Journals = Mutuals-Only Posting.

More Improvements

This update also brings a handful of quality-of-life improvements across the app:

  • Thread-smart notifications - tapping a notification for replies, comments, awards, or thanks now takes you straight to the relevant thread.
  • Revised notifications - cleaner, clearer, and better at surfacing what matters most to you.
  • And many more improvements under the hood.

We're just getting started. Thank you for growing with us and for being part of this community. We love all of you.

Keep huddling!

- The Huddle Team
