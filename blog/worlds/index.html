Product Update April 18, 2026

Introducing Worlds — dedicated spaces for your fandom

🎮

Fandoms have always deserved a real home — not a scattered group chat, not a hashtag, not a subreddit bolted onto a platform that doesn't really understand them. Today, we're taking the biggest step yet toward building that home.

We're introducing Worlds — dedicated spaces built entirely around a single fandom. Whether you're into Elden Ring speedruns, BLACKPINK comebacks, or every frame of Demon Slayer, your World is yours.

What's inside a World

Each World comes with custom channels so your community can split conversations by topic — lore, clips, memes, spoilers — without everything collapsing into one noisy feed. You can set roles, welcome new fans, and shape the vibe exactly how you want it.

Worlds are discoverable. Fans can search by fandom, browse what's active, and join in seconds. No invite link required. No gatekeeping.

Available now

Worlds are live today on iOS and Android. Open Huddle, tap Explore, and find your World — or create one and be the first to plant your flag.

This is just the beginning. More World features are coming over the next few weeks — we'll keep you posted right here.

— The Huddle Team
