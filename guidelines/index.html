Community Guidelines

Last updated April 18, 2026

Be kind

Huddle is built for fans. Treat every member with respect, even when you disagree. Harassment, threats, and hate speech are not allowed under any circumstances.

Keep it real

Don't impersonate other users, celebrities, or brands. Don't spread misinformation. Share content you have the right to share.

Stay on topic

Each World has a focus. Respect that focus and the community that built it. Spam and off-topic self-promotion will be removed.

Report, don't retaliate

If you see something that breaks these rules, report it. Don't engage or escalate — let the moderation team handle it. Reach us at hello@huddle.so
